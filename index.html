Refreshing Kenya

We are actively supporting government’s health objectives by diversifying our portfolio of beverages to give consumers a greater choice of beverages and the information they need to make healthy decisions about a balanced diet. This includes our efforts to introduce a greater variety of nutritiously enhanced products, such as fruit juices and value-added dairy products, as well as our efforts to rethink many of our recipes to provide no- and low- sugar options to our consumers.

Purpose and vision

Refresh the world. Make a difference
Our vision is to craft the brands and choice of drinks that people love, to Refresh them in body & spirit. And done in ways that create a more sustainable business and better shared future that makes a difference in people's lives, communities and our planet.
